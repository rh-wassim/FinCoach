## Full Manual Demo
**Step 1** — Register  
Go to `/register`, create account (name, email, password)

**Step 2** — Empty dashboard  
Login → `/dashboard` → shows "No data yet" empty state

**Step 3** — Import CSV  
Go to `/transactions` → drag & drop or upload `demo-transactions.csv`  
Success: "X transactions imported successfully"

**Step 4** — Dashboard KPIs  
Return to `/dashboard`, select month **2025-10**  
Show: Total Income (€3 250), Total Expenses, Balance, Savings Rate

**Step 5** — Charts  
- Pie chart: spending by category (Logement, Alimentation, Transport…)  
- Bar chart: monthly evolution (last 6 months)

**Step 6** — Recommendations  
Scroll down → AI-generated tips based on real spending data

**Step 7** — Chatbot  
Go to `/assistant`  
Ask: *"Combien j'ai dépensé ?"*  
Ask: *"Quelle est ma catégorie la plus dépensière ?"*  
Ask: *"Quel est mon solde ?"*

**Step 8** — Savings Goal  
Go to `/goals` → Create goal "Vacances été", €500, future deadline  
Show progress bar at 0% → click "Add funds" → enter amount
